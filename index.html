Getting Started with Trezor

Welcome to Trezor.io/start! Here you will find all the necessary steps to get your Trezor device up and running. Follow these instructions to secure your digital assets:

  1. Unbox your Trezor device and connect it to your computer.
  2. Download and install the Trezor Suite software from the official Trezor website.
  3. Open Trezor Suite and follow the on-screen instructions to set up your device.
  4. Create a secure PIN and write down your recovery seed. Store it in a safe place.
  5. Once setup is complete, you can start managing your cryptocurrencies securely.

For more detailed instructions and troubleshooting, visit the Trezor Support page.
